What to visit

Walk through the streets of the village and let yourself be fascinated by its stone houses built between the giant boulders and rocks of the mountain. Or go up to the castle.

Where to eat

"Manteigas" is the restaurant to go to. You have to try the local delicacy: Polvo Ã  Lagareiro, a dish of octopus, potatoes and olive oil. It will leave you crying for more! Or just discover the local sweets and wines.

Where to stay

"Casa das Jardas" guest house offers the best of rural tourism, in an isolated, quiet location, with a swimming pool and a farm with its beautiful fruit trees.

A village of dragons

Yes, HBO's "House of the Dragon" was filmed here. Monsanto was chosen as the setting for Dragonstone, the home of the Tagaryen family. You can visit the filming locations such as the castle or the abandoned chapel.
